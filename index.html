Home Energy

How long do you use electricity at home, school and outside your house? Input numbers from 1-24 as HOUR/S. If 30 minutes or less, input 0.5.
Ex. I charged my phone for about an hour, and watched television for 2 hours straight. Thereefore my Electricity Consumption equates to 3 hours.

Enter 0 if not applicable. If left blank, will default to 0.

How long do you use water on your daily basis? Input numbers from 1-24 as HOUR/S. If 30 minutes or less, input 0.5.
Ex. I take a bath for around 30 minutes and wash dishes for every meal in 10 minutes; with an average of 30 minutes combined for the 3 meals. Therefore my Water Consumption equates to an hour.

Enter 0 if not applicable. If left blank, will default to 0.
