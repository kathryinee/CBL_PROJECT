The dedicated team behind CalculATE is comprised of Katherine Manaig and Dominique Padilla as the main developers of the calculator and website, Frans De Justo and Reese Gollod as the informations team, and Lyn Rosario and Zeu You as the creatives team. As senior high school students from DLSU-IS brought together with the common goal of educating the public, the CalculATE website is the finished output of all the students' collective efforts.
