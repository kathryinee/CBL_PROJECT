GOALS

What do you need to know about Carbon Footprint?

  • Carbon footprint is the accumulated amount of greenhouse gasses per person. These greenhouse gasses can range from carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrous oxide. Each individual's carbon footprint can range from high to low, depending on their daily greenhouse gas emissions.

    What are the Ways to Reduce Your Carbon Footprint?

      By making changes to our habits and adding new practices to our routine, we can reduce our Carbon Footprint. Simple actions that we can incorporate in our daily lives that can significantly lessen our Carbon Footprint are as follows:

    • Deleting emails and unnecessary data in the cloud.
    • Unplugging unused electronics.
    • Conserving Water.
    • Limiting and Recycling our Wastes.
    • Use of Public Transport.
    • Consuming Local and Seasonal Products.
    • Buying secondhand or responsibly-made clothes.
